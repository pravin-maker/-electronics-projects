# 🔍 Circuit Exploration

## 📌 Description
This project focuses on exploring and analysing different electronic circuit boards by opening electronic devices and studying the internal components and circuit design.

## 🔎 What I Explore
- Identifying ICs and electronic components
- Understanding power supply circuits
- Observing PCB layout and design
- Learning how different electronic devices work internally

## 📷 Activities
- Opening electronic devices
- Studying the circuit board
- Identifying components like resistors, capacitors, ICs, and regulators
- Understanding how the circuit functions
